About Our Company

W. R. Boansal and O' Connor India. An associate company of S. A. O' Connors Inc.,USA. One of the largest producers of speciality chemicals for the building industry in the state of Florida, USA. Presence in more than thirteen countries across the world.

The company was established in 2001 in Chennai and we were importing and marketing paint products from the US. We started our manufacturing Fecility at Chennai in 2007 to produce powder putty, paste putty,texture coatings, primers and water based emulsions of all grades. Our total production capacity is 10296 TPA. We can achieve a turnover of 120 crore with the available capacity of our existing plant.

We have been supplying and doing the application works for major Developeres in south India ( please refer our website for the list of our customers)
